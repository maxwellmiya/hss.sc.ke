At Highway Senior School, our boarding programme is designed to provide a safe, structured, and enriching environment for every student. We believe that the boarding experience is integral to developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared for life's challenges.

Our dedicated team of house masters, matrons, and support staff work around the clock to ensure every student's physical, emotional, and social wellbeing. We maintain high standards of discipline, hygiene, and nutrition while creating space for recreation, personal growth, and meaningful connections.
